Game: Reverse Minesweeper, a variant on an old game

Manu Raghavan
2016-06-02

Reverse Minesweeper is a variant on the original game.

The objective is the same as in the original, mark all mines and don’t click on any. But, there’s a twist – all the boxes are open; mines and open spaces look alike. Mark all mines to finish the game.
