VoIP PBX software stack: SIP switch, media mixers, web + iOS + Android softphone clients

Voxology was a VoIP phone system for international business teams. As of 2017, it is no longer online.

Voxology allowed teams to be more productive by integrating phone calls into the apps that are already critical parts of their business workflow, like CRM and helpdesk software suites.
